The Hotel & Metalwalkway

Welcome to Cloud 9 Antipolo's Hotel and Flowery Metalwalkway – a harmonious blend of luxury accommodation and thrilling adventure nestled in the heart of Antipolo, Philippines.

Step into a realm of opulence and tranquility at our hotel, where every detail is meticulously curated to ensure a lavish and comfortable stay. From elegantly designed rooms boasting stunning views of the surrounding landscapes to impeccable service and top-notch amenities, your experience with us promises to be nothing short of extraordinary.

But the excitement doesn't end there. Embark on a journey through nature unlike any other as you traverse the Flowery Metalwalkway of Cloud 9. Suspended amidst a lush canopy of trees, this enchanting pathway invites you to immerse yourself in a botanical wonderland. Adorned with vibrant flowers and verdant foliage, the Metalwalkway offers a sensory feast for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike.

Discover the perfect synergy of luxury and adventure at Cloud 9 Antipolo's Hotel and Flowery Metalwalkway. Plan your escape today and prepare to be enchanted by an experience that seamlessly combines indulgence with exhilaration.
